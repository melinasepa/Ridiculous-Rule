SAM WAS HERE

The Most Ridiculous Rule

It began at St. Thomas the Apostle, my catholic grade school.

We had to walk in a huge circle, moving right, to get to the next class even if it was just across the hall!

Most Annoying Part...

I remember that my first class each morning was science, located directly across the hall from my homeroom. I always hated having to loop around the entire floor just to get to class.
